# Board Pitch

## Thesis (1 sentence)

AI Lifestyle Companion becomes the trusted daily health coach that helps users improve lifestyle habits through personalized, longitudinal coaching powered by behavioral data.

## The case

Most people know how to live healthier but struggle to sustain behavior change. Existing health and fitness apps provide information but fail to create long-term accountability and personalized guidance.

## Why now

Generative AI enables affordable, personalized coaching at scale while consumers increasingly seek preventative health solutions and digital wellness support.

## What's defensible

The long-term behavioral profile, coaching history, and outcome data generated through continuous user engagement create a proprietary data asset that improves recommendations over time and increases switching costs.

## The economics

Freemium acquisition combined with subscription revenue creates attractive margins due to low incremental AI costs. As retention improves, customer lifetime value expands significantly while AI costs remain relatively stable.

## The risks

User retention may be lower than expected. Users may not perceive sufficient value versus existing health apps. Platform players such as Apple, Google, or OpenAI could launch competing solutions.

## Trust / failure modes

The system does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace healthcare professionals. Confidence signaling, human escalation paths, and safety guardrails reduce the risk of harmful recommendations.

## Scale / governance

The platform follows privacy-by-design principles, GDPR compliance, EU AI Act readiness, human oversight for high-risk situations, and model portability through an abstraction layer.

## Competitive

Competes with wellness apps, habit trackers, and emerging AI health assistants. Differentiation comes from continuous behavioral coaching, outcome tracking, and personalized lifestyle guidance.

## The ask

Invest in validating retention, expanding behavioral data collection, and building the data flywheel required to establish a durable competitive advantage.
